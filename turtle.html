Western Pond Turtle Collaborative

Creative Media

Overview:

This project documents my work with the Western Pond Turtle Collaborative, where I supported conservation efforts through visual communication. I created digital illustrations and merchandise designs to help share the project's mission, and assisted as a photographer during field days, capturing on-site research and conservation efforts.

Western Pond Turtle Collaborative:

The Western Pond Turtle Collaborative is led by various California-based organizations dedicated to protecting Western Pond turtles through research. Partners include: East Bay Regional Park District, Oakland Zoo, SF Zoo, Alameda County Resource Conservation District, Swaim Biological, Sonoma State University, Dominican University, and New Mexico State University.
