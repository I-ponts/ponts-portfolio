who am i ?

I am a designer exploring the space between imagination and function. My background is in creating experiences, objects, and systems that ask “what if?” and push creative boundaries, often at the intersection of nature and technology. Lately, I've been diving into web development to bring these ideas to life through interactive, digital experiences. I'm always learning, always experimenting, and always looking for thoughtful ways to merge design, technology, and the natural world.
