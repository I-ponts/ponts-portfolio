I graduated from UC San Diego with a B.A. in Speculative Design, where I focused on combining creative thinking with hands-on making and user-centered design. Since then, I've contributed to collaborative projects like public art restoration, worked in small business environments that required flexibility and teamwork, and started learning front-end web development to bring interactive ideas to life. I'm especially drawn to sustainable design and creating work that's both intriguing and impactful.
